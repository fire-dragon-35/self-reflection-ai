# Reflektion

**AI-powered self-reflection for understanding your patterns and personality.**

---

## What is Reflektion?

Reflektion helps you understand yourself through conversations with AI. Reflect on your thoughts, explore your patterns, and discover insights about your personality and how you relate to others.

**Not therapy. Not journaling. Self-knowledge.**

## How it works

1. **Chat** - Talk to AI about your day, relationships, anxieties, or anything on your mind
2. **Analyze** - AI detects patterns in your conversations and generates personality insights
3. **Learn** - See your Big Five scores, attachment style, and how they evolve over time
4. **Grow** - Use insights to understand yourself better and make positive changes
